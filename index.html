Welcome to Short Quotes: Inspire Everyday, a motivational quotes application provided by Short Quotes Inc. ("we," "us," or "our"). 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use Short Quotes: Inspire Everyday:

  • Name: Collected via Google Sign-In to personalize your experience and display your username in the app.
  • Email Address: Collected via Google Sign-In to manage your account, send updates, and communicate with you.
  • Profile Preferences: Optional information you provide to customize your experience (e.g., quote themes or categories).
  • Location (Optional): If you choose to share your location, we may use it to offer localized content or features.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-personal device data (e.g., device type, operating system) to improve app performance.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized analytics (e.g., quote interaction rates) to enhance app functionality and user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalize your Short Quotes: Inspire Everyday experience (e.g., display your username, tailor content).
  • Manage your account and send you updates, notifications, or support messages.
  • Improve our app through anonymized analytics and performance data.
  • Offer region-specific features if you share your location.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following cases:

  • Google Sign-In: Your name and email are processed via Google’s authentication service to enable account creation.
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party services (e.g., analytics providers) that process anonymized data to improve our app. These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

5. Data Storage and Security

We store your data securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls. Data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no system is completely secure.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data through the app’s settings or by contacting us.
  • Opt-Out: Opt out of optional data collection (e.g., location) via app settings.

If you are in the EU, UK, or California, you may have additional rights under GDPR or CCPA, such as data portability or correction. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Third-Party Services

Short Quotes: Inspire Everyday uses Google Sign-In for authentication. Google may collect and process your name and email according to its own Privacy Policy. We may also use third-party analytics tools that collect anonymized data to improve our services.

8. Children’s Privacy

Short Quotes: Inspire Everyday is rated for users aged 3 and older (3+ age group). We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other applicable regulations. For users under 13, we collect only minimal personal information necessary to provide the app’s core functionality (e.g., username via Google Sign-In with parental consent). Parents or guardians can contact us to review or delete their child’s data. If we learn that personal information from a child under 13 has been collected without verifiable parental consent, we will delete it promptly.
